Frequently Asked Questions

How does WSpy work?
WSpy is installed directly on the target Android or iOS device. Once activated, it operates in stealth mode, sending data to your secure online dashboard. You can then monitor activities remotely from any web browser.
Is WSpy legal to use?
WSpy is designed for legal monitoring purposes, such as parental control or employee monitoring on company-owned devices. It's the user's responsibility to ensure they have the right to monitor the target device and comply with local laws.
Can WSpy be detected on the target device?
No, WSpy operates in complete stealth mode. It doesn't appear in the app list, doesn't have an icon, and doesn't send any notifications. The user won't know it's installed.
How many devices can I monitor with one license?
Each license is for one device. However, we offer discounts for multiple device monitoring. Contact our sales team for custom packages for businesses or families.
Do I need physical access to the target device?
Yes, you need physical access to the target device for initial installation. After that, all monitoring can be done remotely through your online dashboard.
Does WSpy work on both Android and iOS?
Yes, WSpy is compatible with both Android and iOS devices. No root or jailbreak is required for basic features, but some advanced features may require root access on Android.
How often does WSpy update the monitored data?
WSpy updates monitored data in real-time or near real-time, depending on the target device's internet connection. Most data is available within minutes of the activity occurring.
Is my monitored data secure?
Yes, all data is transmitted and stored using military-grade encryption. Only you have access to your dashboard, and we do not share any data with third parties.
What happens if the target device is factory reset?
If the target device is factory reset, WSpy will be uninstalled along with all other data and apps. You would need to reinstall WSpy after the reset.
Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
Yes, we offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with WSpy for any reason, contact our support team within 14 days of purchase for a full refund.
